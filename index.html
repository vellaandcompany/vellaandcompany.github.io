About

Our vision is to dominate the market in providing “affordable luxury” housing product to people in New York City and its submarkets, mainly Hoboken and Jersey City. By acquiring underutilized properties and rehabilitating them to make for-sale and for-rent product available to the demanding market. These spaces have had an influx of luxury development, in recent years. Meanwhile, we have seen tremendous demand in the “middle-market” space with little attempt to fulfill the need. New York City is a global hub and part of the roots of American life and business. Manhattan, the outer boroughs, and New Jersey are vibrant landscapes people want to be a part of. We are committed to providing customers with space that is efficient and modern so they can enjoy the live/work/play environment we call home.

We further put a great emphasis on the value of our investors and shareholders. Our financial mission is to create tremendous value by capitalizing on fantastic opportunities in these markets to achieve strong, risk-adjusted returns. By working to produce the less costly alternatives in our markets we are taking on a niche segment which will prove to be very profitable. We are further able to hedge against risk by acquiring existing properties which 1) can provide some rental income during repositioning and 2) the fact that we can hold these properties as rentals or convert to condominiums, give us staying power in the case of unexpected occurrences (i.e. market downturn), 3) our capital stack will have relatively low leverage. Investment decisions are based on the fundamentals of the acquisition and on our strategy for the property so as to create a value for investors and make our mark on the community.

We truly believe that if you take care of your customers, they will take care of you. This drives us to bring a high quality product to the market and give our customers a little more than expected. Our customers are our number one priority.
