About

Based in Hoboken, and real estate investors for over 25 years,we are a real estate investment and development company with a strong focus on developing and rehabilitating residential properties in Hudson County. Our simple idea is to increase the supply of high quality housing in the supply-constrained New York Metropolitan market. Our firm consists of a team of professionals with expertise in all facets of the real estate development business as well as deep relationships throughout our market.

Principals

Mark Vella
          Mark’s understanding and ability in the fields of Finance, Construction, and Real Estate tie this firm together and bring integration. He graduated from the Stillman School of Business at Seton Hall University with a major in Finance. His real estate background comes in the form of brokerage and development. Becoming a real estate agent at a young age and working with tremendous leaders in the Hoboken and Jersey City markets, he has developed a keen sense of value in the real estate business.

Anthony Vella
          Anthony has a tremendous knowledge of construction and design. He began apprenticing at his father’s New York City construction business at a very young age. After graduating from Hofstra University with a Bachelor’s of Science in Mechanical Engineering he began working as a mechanical engineer for the U.S. Army. Throughout his career he has completed a number of successful real estate ventures in New York and New Jersey.

Dean Clark
          Dean’s knowledge and expertise in Real Estate is second to none. Drawing from years of experience as a sales professional and a finance background, he was able to launch himself into the Real Estate business with ease. Dean has extensive knowledge of the different cities and neighborhoods in Northern New Jersey and has a vast knowledge of the City and the ins and outs of new developments along the Hudson. His determination, ambition and creativity are vital parts of his make-up, enabling him to facilitate over $40 million in sales in 2016 alone. Dean is a real estate agent with Remax on the Hudson in Hoboken, New Jersey.

Mark Schild
          Mark is an exceptional finance professional having started his career at Drexel Lambert in 1984 as an investment broker and advisor. Soon on starting Schild Asset Management, he successfully grew the firm’s assets under management to $500 million. Mark is currently the President of Stonecrest Partners a national broker-dealer and registered investment advisor. He is also an adjunct professor at Seton Hall University and a graduate of Tulane University with a Bachelor’s of Science in Management.

Acquisition Criteria

Vella & Company is seeking opportunities with the following criteria:
  -Value-Add Multifamily and Mixed-Use Buildings and Development Sites/Land
  -Located in Hudson County
  -Required information: Property Address, Purchase Price, Current Tenancy, etc.
  -Please email opportunities which fit this criteria to acquisitions@vellacompany.com

Acquisition Criteria #1

Property Type: Land/Knockdown
Price Range: $50,000-$2,000,000 (any size in Hoboken considered)
Location: Hudson County, Essex County, Union County, Passaic County
Deal Type: Residential/Multifamily or Mixed-Use development

Acquisition Criteria #2

Property Type: Multifamily and Mixed-Use Buildings
Price Range: $1,000,000-$5,000,000
Size: 20+ units (any size in Hoboken considered)
Location: Hudson County, Essex County, Union County, Passaic County
Deal Type: value-add or stabilized
